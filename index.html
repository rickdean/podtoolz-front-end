Why start a podcast?

If you have something you are passionate about and would like to share there is no better way than with a podcast. It's personal, powerful, and accessible.

Is it expensive?

There has been no better time to produce a podcast. Especially when it comes to producing a quality show without having to invest a lot of money.

About This Site

I've been asked many times what is needed to start a podcast. The good news is that it's not as much as you think. That is why I created Podtoolz. I've put together a list of the equipment I use and also some very inexpensice (and sometimes free!) alternatives. Once you've checked off the items you are interested in you can print your list and start shopping! My name is Rick and I've been producing shows since 2012. Projects include 'Evolution Talk' and, as of 2016, I've been writing, directing, and producing audio-dramas such as The Behemoth, Bryar Lane, and Scotch.
